Our Life in IEEE

IEEE Curtin Sarawak work hard to bring a fulfilling university experience to the students
and members. Many Events and activities are conducted successfully in the past.
More great things are yet to come.

Advanced Arduino Workshop 2017

Every year, IEEE organizes arduino workshops for IEEE members and non-members. Advanced arduino workshops are created for those who wish to learn more from basic arduino workshops.  Afterall, innovation is what drives them forward.

Project Exhibition

IEEE members always come out with mysterious and interesting projects. That is why we let those who are keen to innovate and showcast their projects on exhibition day. Anything you can imagine and build. What will you make out of it?

Site Visit in Kuching 2016

Having site visits help IEEE members to gain broader insights on what is in store in the outside world. This year, IEEE curtin sarawak has made a site visit to X-FAB sarawak in Kuching.

IEEEXtreme 10.0 2016

IEEEXtreme 10.0 is just as extreme as it sounded. International members from universities all around the world challenges against head scratching programming questions under 24 hours. Curtin Sarawak happens to have some daredevils participating in this event.

IEEE Night 2016

IEEE Members and non-members had an exhilarating evening in IEEE Night organized by Curtin IEEE student branch in Curtin Sarawak. Exciting activities happened that night are games,  microphone sessions, performances and member inductions.

Solar Bus Stop

Do you remember those nights when you just have to go to those dark bus stops? We are happy to let you know, with the recent installment of solar powered lights to the bus stops near Curtin Sarawak by the IEEE members, the nights now are light up as bright as the day at the bus stops.

Tech Mentor Workshop

Tech Mentor Workshop is about preparing mentors to guide high school student for Arduino Programming. These mentors are trained with the basic of Arduino Programming, they are needed to pass on the knowledge to the high school students and guide them to produce an Arduino Project.

Team Building

Team building is an event for IEEE committees to strengthen the bond between each other. The committees can know more about each other during the event. Some interesting activities were happened such as games sessions, funny Q n A session and others.

Boot Camp 2017

Boot camp is a advanced Arduino workshop for the students from secondary schools. It is prepared for them in order to know more about the basic knowledge of Arduino. After that, the students will create their own projects according the challenges they chose and show thiere projects during Young Innovate Challenge.
