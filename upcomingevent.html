News & Upcoming Events

Arduino Workshop
4th March 2017

This Arduino Workshop was conducted by Mr.Sim. During the workshop, the students were learning about the basic Arduino, including capacitive touch, LCD display, IR remote control, theremine and also the combination of different components. Mr.Sim was paying a lot of patience on the students to solve their problems. This Arduino Workshop was really a great experience for the students.

Club Fair
30th - 31st March 2017

Organized by Student Council, the Club Fair highlights the university's campus diversity and introduces new students to various clubs and organizations on campus. Being one of the university's largest events, the fair demonstrates how student participation in the student-led organizations on campus helps them pursue their personal interests and enthusiasm outside the classroom, as well as building leadership skills.

Welcoming Party
31st March 2017

Welcoming Party is mainly for the new member and non-member to know more about IEEE, and also to strengthen the bond between the committees and members. This welcoming party was being held in SK3 103. During the event, the advisors and president were introducing to the new members and non-members about IEEE. There were some funny activities for them and also free dinner was provided.

Bootcamp
31st March - 1st April 2017

The bootcamp was being held for the secondary students to know more about the basic of Arduino. During the bootcamp, the mentors guided their own school team. After the bootcamp, the students will have to build their own projects according to the chosen challenge and participate in a competition called YIC. This event was being held in the Skylark building.
