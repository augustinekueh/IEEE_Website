Solar Bus Station
Posted Mar 27, 2016

IEEE Curtin Sarawak Student Branch proudly to have successfully uphold its vision of Advancing Technology for Humanity. Recently, the club ran a Solar-powered lighting system for a Curtin Accommodation Bus Stop for two consecutive years. The Energy stored by the Panel during the day would be used by the LEDs at the Bus-stop to brighten up the place, providing students safety when walking back to their accommodation at night. Maintenence have been done to keep the lights shining bright. Say "NO" to vandalism.
